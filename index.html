Synopsis

Continent

Empires And Acadamy

Miron Empire

The Miron Empire is Nor Verner’s homeland and the wealthiest empire on the continent. Its power comes from enormous deposits of mana ore, a rare resource used in artifacts, weapons, formations, and cultivation. Thanks to these riches and its major trade ports, Miron has become one of the most influential forces in the world.

Kreyton Emipre

The Creyton Empire is the strongest of the great empires, known for its overwhelming military power, strict discipline, and battle-hardened warriors. It borders the Demonic Beast Territory, which has shaped it into a harsh and highly combat-oriented nation. Though not as wealthy as Miron or as focused on mana research as Dormund, Creyton stands above both in overall combat strength.

Dormund Empire

The Dormund Empire is a great power known for its powerful mages and advanced mana research. It is stronger than the Miron Empire in terms of magical and combat power, but poorer due to fewer resources and still weaker than the Creiton Empire. Ruled by Icarus Daitron, Dormund values talent, mana knowledge, and personal strength above wealth.

Academy Aladen

Aladen Academy is a neutral and incredibly powerful institution located near the border of the Demonic Beast Territory. More like a city than a school, it is protected by massive walls and a faint blue barrier. Its central building is divided into the Magic Division and the Knight Division, with the symbol of scales representing balance, neutrality, and judgment.
