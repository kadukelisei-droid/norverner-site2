Synopsis

Running from the wolf, Nor stumbled and fell. Caught by the beast, he grabbed a stone in desperation and hurled it straight at its head.

From that moment on, his life changed forever.

Will he be able to control the power he was given... or will he become a catastrophe the world has never seen?
