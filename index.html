I Became The Strongest In One Day

Power System

Mana, ranks and elements

Mana

Mana is the mysterious life force that empowers combat, magic, and the soul of the Miron Empire. It can be shaped, felt, and mastered through training and insight.

Ranks

Every cultivator rises through distinct stages. Each rank grants stronger abilities, greater influence, and a deeper connection to elemental power.

Elements

Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, Lightning, Darkness, Light, and more. Elements define a user's style, strengths, and the way they shape the battlefield.

Creation & Destruction

Nor Verner may wield both Creation and Destruction mana, or a unique fusion of both. This duality is the heart of the story's evolving threat.

World

Realm, empire and academy

Miron Empire

A sprawling empire shaped by politics, legend, and the rule of a powerful sovereign. The Miron Empire is home to intrigue, rivalry, and hidden history.

Academy

The central institution for aspiring warriors and mages. Admission is rare, training is brutal, and every student learns that power has a price.

Locations

From capital cities to lost landmarks, this world contains vivid locations that shape the journey of Nor and their allies.
